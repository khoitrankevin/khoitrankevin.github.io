AUTUMN LEAVES

Different types of autumn leaves are identified by the tree species they come from, which dictate their unique colors and forms, including the brilliant scarlet of maples, the golden-yellow of aspens and beeches, and the various reds and oranges of oaks and sumacs. Common examples include the characteristic fan-shaped leaves of the Ginkgo and the multi-colored foliage of Sassafras and Staghorn Sumac.

Colors of autumn leaves: Yellow/ Golden, Red/Purple, Orange/Russet, Mixed Colors, Brown.
